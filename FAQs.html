Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Q1. Is RecruitMate free for job seekers?

Yes! RecruitMate is 100% free for candidates. You can apply to unlimited jobs and check out jobs in demand.


Q2. How can I apply for a job?

After logging in, go to your dashboard, browse the jobs listed, and click the "Apply" button next to any job you're interested in.


Q3. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the steps to reset your password.


Q4. How do I post a job vacancy?

Once logged in as a recruiter, go to the “Post a Job Listing” section and fill in the job details to publish your listing.


Q5. Is there any cost for recruiters to use RecruitMate?

Currently, RecruitMate is free for both candidates and recruiters during the beta phase. Future plans may include premium features.


Q6. Is my data secure on RecruitMate?

Yes. We use secure database storage and follow best practices to keep user information safe and private.


Q7. Who can I contact for technical support or queries?

You can email us using the Contact Us form available on our page.
